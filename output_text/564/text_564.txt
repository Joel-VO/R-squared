ORDER
The petitioners are the accused in Crime No.785/2013
of Kattakkada Police Station registered for the offence
under Section 498A IPC, The first petitioner is the husband
of the 3" respondent and the second petitioner is his mother
and the third petitioner is his sister. It is submitted that the
matter has been settled between the parties and the
proceedings in the criminal case may be quashed.
2. Heard, the learned counsel for the petitioners and
of the 3" respondent and also the learned Public Prosecutor.

3. The third respondent has appeared through a
counsel and filed an affidavit to the effect that the matter
has been settled and she has no objection to the
proceedings in the criminal case being quashed. Having
regard to the nature of the case I am satisfied that this is a

fit case to invoke the jurisdiction of this Court under Section
 201SKERIII74

(CrLM.C.No.6761/2014.

482 Cr.PC.

4, In the result, this petition is allowed. The
proceedings in Crime No,785/2013 of Kattakkada Police
Station are quashed.
