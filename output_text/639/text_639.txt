CRL.M.C. No.7843 of 2015

Petitioners are accused in C.C. No.497/2009 of the
Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-I, North Paravoor,
which has arisen from Crime No.804/2006 of the North
Paravoor Police Station, registered for the offences
punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 324, 447 and 506
(ii) read with Section 149 of the Indian Penal Code.

2. The matter has been amicably settled between
the petitioners and the defacto complainant, who is the
second respondent herein and the other affected parties,
who are respondents 2 to 13. They have filed separate
affidavits as Annexures A2 to A13 affirming that the matter
has been amicably settled between them and the petitioners

and they have no complaints against the petitioners.
 CRLM.C.No, 7843 of 2015

3. No criminal antecedents have been reported
against the petitioners. When the matter has been amicably
settled, no purpose would be served in proceeding with the
matter further, and therefore, all further proceedings in
C.C. No.497/2009 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's
Court-!, North Paravoor, as against the petitioners, based on
Annexure A1 Final Report in Crime No.804/2006 of the
North Paravoor Police Station, can be quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings in C.C. No.497/2009 of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate's Court-I, North Paravoor, as against the
petitioners, based on Annexure A1 Final Report in Crime
No.804/2006 of the North Paravoor Police Station, are

hereby quashed.
