THIS CRIMINAL MISC. CASE HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON
07.01.2020, THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 ORDER
Dated this the 7th day of January 2020

The petitioners are accused 1 to 3 in
C.C.No.1739/2018 on the file of the Judicial First
Class Magistrate Court-I, Hosdurg, for having
allegedly committed the offences punishable under
Sections 143, 147, 148, 448, 427 and 506(ii) read
with Section 149 of I.P.C. The defacto complainant-
injured are respondents 3 to 5 herein, who have
appeared through a Counsel and have filed affidavits
at Annexures A2 to A4 stating that the matter has
been amicably settled and they have no existing
grievance against the petitioners. A report has been
filed by the investigating officer through the Senior
Public Prosecutor to that effect. The petitioners do
not have any criminal antecedents and no public
interest is involved.

In the result, the Crl.M.c. is allowed and the

entire proceedings in Crime No.419/2018 of Hosdurg
 Police Station, now pending as C.C.No.1739/2018 on
the file of the Judicial First Class Magistrate
Court-I, Hosdurg, stands quashed under Section 482 of

Cr.P.C. and the petitioners are discharged.
