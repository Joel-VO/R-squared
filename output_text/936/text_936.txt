JUDGMENT
1, This is a case charge sheeted by the Sub Inspector of Police, Poojappura

Police Station crime No. 856/2024 against the accused alleging commission
of offences w/s 305, 331(3), 334(1), 238 and 13 of Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita.

2. The case of the prosecution as revealed from the final report is that on
19/07/2024 at 12.05 pm, the accused who has criminal antecedents trespassed
into the house of CWI bearing No. 17/200, Melarannoor Goverment
Quarters in Thycaud Village, broke open the door of the house using iron rod
and committed theft of 8.5 sovereigns of gold ornaments worth Rs.4,00,000.
‘Thereafter the accused destroyed evidence in such a manner so as to render
recovery of stolen gold omaments impossible. The accused thereby
committed the offences as alleged against him. It is also reported that the
accused has been convicted in 2013 in a case CC: 3232/2013 (Poojappura

Police Station) and also in 2018 in CC: 476/2018 (Kazhakuttom Police
 3

Station), CC 785/2018 (Kottayam Gandhi Nagar Police Station and also in

CC: 2972019 and CC: 1211/2017 Both of Emakulam Central Police

Station),

Accused is under custody from 07/08/2024. Accused is produced. He is

represented by legal aid defence counsel. He filed petition to plead guilty.

Hence, charge for the offences ws 305, 331(3), 334(1) and 238 of Bharatiya

Nyaya Sanhita was framed, read over and explained to the accused to which

he pleaded guilty. The plea is voluntarily made, with proper legal assistance

and after fully understanding the consequences thereof. The accused is
therefore found guilty of the offences w/s 305, 331(3), 334(1) and 238 of

Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita and is convicted thereunder. Even though, section 13

of BNS is arrayed, the certified copies of relevant judgments are not seen

produced along with the final report, Hence, charge for enhanced punishment

Ws 31 of BNSS is not framed against the accused.

Heard the accused on question of sentence ws 271(2) of BNSS. Accused

submitted that he has nothing to state regarding the sentence. Accordingly the

accused is sentenced as follows:

1. Accused is sentenced to undergo Rigorous Imprisonment for a period of 3
‘years and to pay fine of %50,000/- for the offence punishable w/s 305 of
BNS. In default of payment of fine, he shall undergo Rigorous
Imprisonment for a period of 6 months.

2. Accused is further sentenced to undergo Rigorous Imprisonment for a
period of 3 years and to pay fine of € 25,000/- for the offence ws 331(3)

of BNS. In default of payment of fine, he shall undergo Rigorous
 4
Imprisonment fora period of 4 months.

3. Accused is further sentenced to undergo Rigorous Imprisonment for a
period of 1 year and to pay a fine of Rs.10,000/- forthe offence ws 334(1)
of BNS. In default of payment of fine, he shall undergo Rigorous
Imprisonment for a period of 3 months.

4. Accused is further sentenced to undergo Rigorous Imprisonment for a
period of 1 year and to pay a fine of Rs.10,000/- for the offence ws 238
of BNS. In default of payment of fine, he shall undergo Rigorous
Imprisonment fora period of 3 months.

5. Sentences shall run concurrently, Set off is allowed from 07/08/2024 till
(06/03/2025. Fine amount if realized, shall be paid as compensation to
