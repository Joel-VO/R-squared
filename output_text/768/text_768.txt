ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in Crime
No.614/2017 of Adhur Police Station, registered for the
offences punishable under Sections 341, 323, 324, 294(b),
506 and 308 read with Section 34 IPC.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the petitioners and the de facto
complainant, who is the 2° respondent herein. The 3°
respondent is the other injured.

3. On going through the matter, this Court is
satisfied that there are no ingredients to invite an offence
under Section 308 IPC in the matter. Respondents 2 and 3
have filed Annexures-II and Ill affidavits, affirming that the
matter has been amicably settled between them and the

petitioners and presently, they have no complaints against
 (CrL.M.C.8877/2017

the petitioners. When the matter has been amicably settled
between the parties, no purpose would be served in
proceeding with the matter any further. Matters being so,
Annexure-| First Information Report in Crime No.614/2017 of
‘Adhur Police Station and all further proceedings as against
the petitioners in it, can be quashed.

In the result, this Cr.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-I
First Information Report in Crime No.614/2017 of Adhur
Police Station and all further proceedings as against the
petitioners in it, are quashed.
