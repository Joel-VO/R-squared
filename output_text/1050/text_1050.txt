This case having been heard on today the court on the same day passed the

ORDER

1. Accused stands charged with the offences punishable wss 296(b), 351(2),
75(1)(ii)(iv), 79, 3(5) Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita.

2. Prosecution case in brief is as follows: On 14.08.2024, at 16.30 hours,
during an altercation between the informant and mother of CCL1 and CCL2 at
the pathway that situated near Beemapally junction, CCL1 and CCL3 who
reached the spot along with the above accused, intervened CCL1 among them
by uttering obscene words against the informant shown his private part to her
with an intention to insult her modesty. It is alleged that accused no.2
threatened to kill her. Thus the accused committed an offence punishable w/ss.
296(b), 351(2), 75(1)(ii)iv), 79, 3(6) Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita,

3. On filing final report, the case was taken on file as CP No. 5/2025. When
process served, the accused person entered appearance. He was enlarged on
 2082

bail during crime stage and allowed to continue in the same. Copies of all
prosecution records were furnished him as provided under section 230 of
Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023.

4. On perusal of the case records, it appears to me that the offences alleged against
the accused is exclusively triable by the Hon’ble Sessions Court. Hence, the
case is to be committed to the Hon'ble Sessions Court, Thiruvananthapuram.
Therefore, the case is hereby committed to the Hon’ble Sessions Court,
‘Thiruvananthapuram ws.232 of Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023.

5. Issue notice to the Public Prosecutor. Notify him of the committal of the case
and transmit records to the Hon’ble Sessions Court as per Rules.

6. The accused shall appear before the Hon'ble Court of Sessions,
‘Thiruvananthapuram on issuance of summons.
