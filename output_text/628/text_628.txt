ORDER

Presently, when allegations are there, there is
absolutely nothing to quash the proceedings in
CC.808/2015 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-I,
Kozhikode. At the same time, it is open to the petitioner to
move the court below under Section 239 Cr.PC., in case
that stage is not over. This Cri.M.C. is disposed of
accordingly.

It has been pointed out that the petitioner has been
implicated in a series of crimes by the police at the instance
of the 3 respondent complainant and his associates. After
the filing of the Cri.m.C., he has been placed under arrest

and presently, he is in custody. The District Police Chief,
 CrL.M.C.7768/2017

Kannur shall see that the petitioner is produced before the
court below in CC.808/2015 on the next posting date for

enabling him to file an application under Section 239 Cr.P.C.
