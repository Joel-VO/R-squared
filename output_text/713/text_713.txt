ORDER

Petitioner was the sixth accused in Crime No.
111 of 1992 of Poonthura Police Station
registered for the offences under Sections 143,
147, 148, 395, 436 and 452 read with Section 149
IPC. After the final report was filed two cases
were taken on the file of the First Additional
Assistant Sessions Judge, Thiruvananthapuram as
S.C.No.65 of 2000 and S.C.No.427 of 2000. As the
petitioner was absconding his case was removed to
the register of long pending cases, the number

being L.P.No.10 of 2003 on the file of the

Judicial Magistrate of ‘First Class-II,
Thiruvananthapuram. The accused who were tried
were acquitted by Annexure A2 Judgment. The

petitioner has now surrendered. His prayer is to
quash the proceedings in the light of the

acquittal of the co-accused.

. Heard the learned counsel for the

petitioner and the learned Public Prosecutor.
 CrL.MC.No. 8594 of 2018 3 2019KERIO

3. I have perused Annexure A2 Judgment by
which the accused who were tried were acquitted.
I am satisfied that no purpose will be served by

the trial of the petitioner.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed. The
case re filed from L.P.No.10 of 2003 on the file
of the Judicial Magistrate of First Class-II,
Thiruvananthapuram is quashed so far as the

petitioner is concerned.
