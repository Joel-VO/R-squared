COMMON ORDER

[ Crl.mc.8401/2019, Crl.Mc.8407/2019 ]
Dated this the 7th day of January 2020

Petitioners in these cases are accused Nos.2
and 1 respectively, in Crime No.845/2011 of
varkkala Police station for having allegedly
committed offences punishable under sections 143,
147, 148, 447, 294(b), 341, 324 and 308 read with
section 149 IPC. The cases against the
petitioners were committed and are presently
pending as SC No.1875/2014 and sc No.543/2013,
respectively, before the Assistant Sessions Court,
Attingal. The defacto complainant/injured is the
second respondent, who has appeared through
counsel and filed separate affidavits in these
cases to the effect that the matter has been
amicably settled with the petitioners and that he
has no grievance against them. Even through
offence under Section 308 IPC has been

incorporated, the injuries are not life
 2020KERISIS

threatening. A report has been obtained from the
investigating officer to the effect that the
matter has been settled. The petitioners do not
have any criminal antecedents. No public interest
is involved.

In the result, these Criminal M.cs are allowed
and the entire proceedings as against the
petitioners pending before the Assistant Sessions
Court, Attingal in SC Nos.1875/2014 and 543/2013
shall stand quashed under Section 482 cr.PC and

the petitioners/accused are discharged.
