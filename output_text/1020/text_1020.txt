Heard, perused the records and passed the following:

JUDGMENT

1. This case has arisen on a final report filed by the Sub Inspector of Police, Thampanoor

Police Station in crime No, 896/2022 alleging the commission of offence against the
 accused under Section 118(a) of Kerala Police Act.

. Accused no. 2 pleaded guilty. Charge was framed under Section 118(a) of
Kerala Police Act was read over and explained to the accused and he pleaded
guilty. This court is convinced that the plea of the accused is voluntary. Hence
his plea is accepted. The accused no. 2 is found guilty for the offence
ws. 118(a) of Kerala Police Act and he is convicted thereunder.

. Accused no. 2 is convicted and sentenced to pay fine of Rs.500/- for the offence
punishable w/s.118(a) of Kerala Police Act. In default of payment of fine, accused
shall undergo simple imprisonment for a period of 10 days. Case against accused
no. 1 and 3 is refiled as CC.181/2025.
