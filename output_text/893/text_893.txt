This case having been heard today, the court delivered the following:
 2
JUDGMENT

The accused was put on trial for the offences punishable under section
294(b) and 323 of the Indian Penal Code.

2. The prosecution’s case in brief is as follows:- The accused, at
about 08.40 p.m., on 14/03/2020, voluntary caused hurt to the informant
and uttered obscene words to the annoyance of the informant while the
informant was traveling in the Rajadhani Express train. The accused
thereby committed the the offences punishable under section 294(b) and 323
of the Indian Penal Code.

3. On the basis of the charge sheet submitted by the Sub
Inspector of Police, Railway Police Station, Thiruvananthapuram,
cognizance was taken for the offences punishable under section 294(b) and
323 of the Indian Penal Code. Summons was issued to the accused. He
appeared before the court. He was enlarged on bail. A copy of the
prosecution records was served on the accused. The particulars of the
offences punishable under section 294(b) and 323 of the Indian Penal Code
are read and explained in Malayalam to which, the accused pleaded not
guilty. Thereafter the accused absconded. So coercive steps were issued
against him. The police arrested the accused and produced him before
this court. He filed an application to read over the particulars of offence
once again. The application was allowed. The particulars of offences are
again read over and explained to the accused to which he pleaded guilty.
‘The plea of guilt was accepted as it was a voluntary one. The accused is
found guilty of the offences punishable under section 294(b) and 323 of
the Indian Penal Code.
 3

In the result, the accused is convicted and sentenced under section
252 of the Cr-P.C., to pay fine of %.500/- (five hundred rupees only) for
the offence punishable under section 294(b) of the Indian Penal Code and in
default of payment of fine, he shall undergo simple imprisonment for 5 days
and to pay fine of %.1000/- (one thousand rupees only) for the offence
able under section 323 of the Indian Penal Code and in default of
