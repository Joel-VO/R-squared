1. Accused stands charged withthe offences punishable ws. 294(b), 341, 452, 324 and
308 r/w. 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2. Prosecution case in brief is as follows:-. Due to previous animosity towards
CWI, accused persons with an intention to assault CW1, on 27.06.2016, at
8.00 hours, at Kripalayam House, Sunami Colony, Valiyaveli, Poundkadavu
Ward, Attipra Village, accused No.1 uttered obscene words towards CW2 and
pushed her down to the ground. Further, accused Nos.1 and 2 criminally
trespassed into the hall room of Kripalayam House with weapons and beat
‘CWI with an iron pipe. Accused No.1 beat CWI with a dangerous iron pipe,
‘when he evaded the same, it caused injury on his above of his left ear and back
side of his head. Accused No.2 wrapped around CW1's waist and restrained
him, Accused No.1 again beat CW1 with an iron pipe, he evaded the same
with his right hand causing injury, swelling and contusion on his wrist and
deep injury on left side of his head. If he did not evade the same, said beat

‘Thiruvananthapuram wherein it was taken on file as C.C. 3005/2016. Thereafter,
was transferred to this court by virtue of the order from the Hon'ble Chief Judicial
