This is a proceedings instituted under Section 482 of
the Code of Criminal Procedure for quashing Annexure - 2 final
report and all further proceedings pursuant thereto now
pending as ST No.2268 of 2018 on the files of the Court of the

Judicial Magistrate of First Class, Alathur.

2. The petitioners are accused Nos.1 and 2 in the
said case. The case was one instituted at the instance of the
third respondent under Sections 294(b), 447 and 323 read with

Section 34 of the Indian Penal Code.

3. Respondents 3 to 5 are the victims of the
aforesaid crime, It is seen that respondents 3 to 5 had settled
their disputes with the petitioners and affidavits to that effect

have been produced as Annexures 3 to 5.

4, Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners,
the leamed Public Prosecutor as also the learned counsel for

respondents 3 to 5.
 2020KERISI
Cr.M.C. No.9283 of 2019 4

5. In the light of the decisions of the Apex
Court in Gian singh v. State of Punjab, (2012) 10 SCC 303,
insofar as the offences alleged against the accused in the case
are only offences punishable under Sections 294(b), 447 and
323 read with Section 34 of the Indian Penal Code, | am of the
view that this is an appropriate case where this court has to
invoke the jurisdiction under Section 482 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure to quash the proceedings against the

petitioners.

In the result, the Crl.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-2
final report and all further proceedings pursuant thereto against
the petitioners pending as ST No.2268 of 2018 on the files of
the Court of the Judicial Magistrate of First Class, Alathur are

quashed.
