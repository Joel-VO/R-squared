This is a proceedings instituted under Section 482 of
the Code of Criminal Procedure for quashing Annexure - A2 final
report and all further proceedings pursuant thereto now
pending as CC No.2374 of 2016 on the files of the Court of the

Judicial Magistrate of First Class - II, Kollam.

2. The petitioner is the sole accused in the said
case. The case was one instituted at the instance of the third
respondent under Sections 506(i) and 294(b) of the Indian
Penal Code and Section 31(1) of the Protection of women from

Domestic Violence Act, 2005

3. It is seen that the third respondent had settled
her disputes with the petitioner and an affidavit to that effect

has been produced as Annexure A3.

4, Heard the learned counsel for the petitioner,
the learned Public Prosecutor as also the learned counsel for

the third respondent.
 0n0iKERI674
Crl.M.C, No.106 of 2020 3

5. In the light of the decision of the Apex Court in
Gian singh v. State of Punjab, (2012) 10 SCC 303, insofar
as the offences alleged against the petitioner are only offences
punishable under Sections 506(i) and 294(b) of the Indian Penal

Code and Section 31(1) of the Protection of women from

Domestic Violence Act, 2005, | am of the view that this is an
appropriate case where this court has to invoke the jurisdiction
under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal Procedure to quash

the proceedings against the petitioner.

In the result, the Crl.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-A2
final report and all further proceedings pursuant thereto against
the petitioner pending as CC No.2374 of 2016 on the files of the
Court of the Judicial Magistrate of First Class - II, Kollam are

quashed.
