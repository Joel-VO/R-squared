ORDER

Petitioners stand arrayed as accused Nos.1 to 3 in Crime

No.918 of 2015 of Pothencode Police Station for offences
punishable under sections 500, 506, 34 and 54C of Indian Penal
Code.

2. The second respondent had married the second
petitioner and their matrimonial relationship got strained. Alleging
various offences against the accused, crime was registered at the
instance of the complainant, who is the second respondent herein
and crime was registered. Pending the proceedings, accused have
approached this Court to quash the proceedings. It is stated that
the parties have resolved their dispute. To substantiate it, second
respondent has affirmed an affidavit produced as Annexure-A3. The
second respondent has reiterated that, she has no objection in
quashing the proceedings.

3. On instructions, learned Public Prosecutor submitted
that the matter was settled and the second respondent has given a
statement to the investigating officer to the effect that the disputes
have been settled and have decided to withdraw the criminal cases

filed against each other. She has also stated that, she has no

objection in quashing Crime No.918 of 2015, if accused Nos.1 to 3
will not misuse the documents allegedly kept by them or used it
against her and her family members. This submission was recorded
and brought to the notice of the petitioners herein.

Accordingly, Crl.M.C is allowed. In the light of the
settlement between the parties, all further proceedings arising from
Crime No.918 of 2015 of Pothencode Police Station will stand
quashed, being a purely person dispute.
