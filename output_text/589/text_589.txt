ORDER

The petitioner is the first accused in Crime No.44/2014
of Eravipuram Police Station registered for the offences
under Sections 323 and 498A IPC. The other accused are
their close relatives, The second respondent is his wife. It
is submitted that the proceedings as against the co-accused
have already been quashed by this Court since the matter
has been settled. The second respondent has entered
appeared through a counsel and filed a petition to the effect
that the matter has been settled between her and the
petitioner and she has no objection to the proceedings in
the criminal case being quashed as against the petitioner
also.

2. Heard, the learned counsel for the petitioner and
the learned Public Prosecutor and perused the petition filed

by the second respondent. 1 am satisfied that the
