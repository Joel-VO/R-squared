ORDER
Dated this the 06" day of January, 2020

The petitioners are accused in Crime.No.333/2018 of
Malappuram Police Station presently pending as
C.C.No.1323/2018 on the files of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate Court, Malappuram for having allegedly committed
offences punishable under Sections 341, 323 and 326 read with
Section 34 of the IPC. The only injured is the 2 respondent who
has appeared through counsel and filed an affidavit to the effect
that the matter has been amicably settled with the petitioners.
He has no grievance against the petitioners. The injury sustained
is not life threatening. The petitioners do not have any criminal
antecedents. A report has been filed by the Investigating Officer

through the learned Public Prosecutor.

In the result, the Crl.MC is allowed and the entire
proceedings in C.C.No.1323/2018 on the files of the Judicial First
Class Magistrate Court, Malappuram stands quashed under

Section 482 of the Cr.P.C and the accused are discharged.
