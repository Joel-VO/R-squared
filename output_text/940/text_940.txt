JUDGMEN

1, Accused stands charged with the offence punishable u/s 27(b) of Narcotic

Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act.

2 Accused entered appearance. Requirements w/s.207 Cr.PC was complied with.
 The particulars of offences were read over and explained to the accused to
which he pleaded guilty. 1 accept the plea since I am convinced of its
genuineness and that the same is made voluntarily. Hence, I find the accused
guilty of the offence punishable w/s.27(b) of NDPS Act and accordingly the
accused is convicted and sentenced to pay a fine of %1000/- for the offence
punishable w/s.27(b) of Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act and in
default; to undergo simple imprisonment for 10 days.
