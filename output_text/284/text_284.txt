ORDER

The petitioners, accused in crime No:714/2011 of
Chavara Police Station for offences under Section 498(A), 323,
324 read with Section 34 of the Indian Penal Code have filed
this petition under Section 482 of the Code of Criminal
Procedure for quashing the proceedings pending against
them, The crime is at present pending as C.C.450/2015 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court, Chavara. The case
of the petitioners is that, the disputes have been amicably
settled between the petitioners and the second respondent.
The Family Cout has also passed a decree of divorce.
Therefore, the petitioners seek the issue of orders quashing

the criminal proceedings that are pending.

2.Adv.A.Mohammed Raffi appears for the second
respondent. The counsel also submits that the disputes have
been settled and that, there is no objection to the proceedings
being quashed. The Public Prosecutor does not voice any
other objections.

This Criminal M.C. Is therefore allowed. All further
proceedings in C.C.450/2015 pending before the Judicial First
Class Magistrate's Court, Chavara are quashed.
