ORDER

The petitioner herein is the accused in Crime No.40/2013 of
Excise Range Office, Thrissur for offence punishable under Section
55(a) of the Abkari Act. He was proposed to has been prosecuted
‘on an allegation of transporting 510 liters of spirit in a vehicle on
09.05.2013. There was no on the spot arrest of the petitioner.
The case against the petitioner herein is now pending as
L.P.No.139/2016 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-Ill,
Thrissur, since procuring his presence in court was unsuccessful.
The petitioner contends that, he was not aware of the
proceedings and had come to know of it only recently. He has
approached this Court seeking relief with an offer that he is ready
and willing to appear before the court below and to seek bail. The
apprehension of the petitioner is that, the bail application is not
likely to be taken up on the same day and he is likely to be
remanded.

2. Having considered these facts, | feel that though the

allegation against the petitioner herein is serious, since essential

attempt is to procure the presence of the accused before the
court of law to face the trial, | feel that a lenient view is liable to
be taken.

Hence, Crl.M.C. is disposed of with a direction that in the
event of the petitioner herein surrendering before the court below
within 10 days from today and filing an application for bail, it shall
be considered by the court below on the same day itself having
regard to the above facts. The warrant, if any, issued by the court
below will be kept in abeyance till the actual date of his

appearance within the above mentioned period.
