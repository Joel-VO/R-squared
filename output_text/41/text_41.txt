ORDER

Petitioner is the fifth accused in Crime No.186 of 2001 of
Kothamangalam Police Station, now pending as C.C.No.517 of
2011 on the file of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court,
Kothamangalam. The offences alleged are punishable under
Sections 143, 147, 148, 323, 324 and 326 r/w Section 149 of
the Indian Penal Code. The Cri. M.C. is filed seeking to quash
all further proceedings against the petitioner in C.C.No.517 of
2011 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court,
Kothamangalam.

the Code of Criminal Procedure. The learned Magistrate on
evidence held that, identity of the accused persons was not

established that, the available materials will not suggest anything
