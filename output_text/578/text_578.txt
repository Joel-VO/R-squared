ORDER

This Cri.M.C is filed by accused Nos.1 and 2 in C.C,
No.265/2016 to quash all further proceedings in Crime No.3449
of 2015 of Perumbavoor police station in C.C. No.265/2016
pending before the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court,
Perumbavoor.

2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and the
learned Public Prosecutor.

3. According to the petitioners, altogether four accused
were charge sheeted in the crime. Accused Nos.1 and 2 filed
this Cr.MC, out of four accused in the case. Hence, the
petitioners seek to withdraw the Crl.M.C so as to file a fresh one
by all the accused in the above case.

‘After having taken into consideration of the submission,
the petitioners are permitted to withdraw the Cri.M.C, without
prejudice to file fresh Crl.M.C, if so advised.

The Cri.M.C is dismissed as withdrawn accordingly.
