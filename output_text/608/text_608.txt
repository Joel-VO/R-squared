ORDER

The petitioners are the accused in CC.No.485/2012 on
the file of the Judicial Magistrate of the First Class,
Malappuram. The first petitioner is the husband of the first
respondent and petitioners 2 and 3 are his parents. The
allegation was that the petitioners subjected the first
respondent to cruelty and misappropriated her properties.
It is submitted that the matter has been settled between the
parties and the proceedings may be quashed.

2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and
the first respondent and the learned Public Prosecutor. The

first respondent has entered appearance through the

counsel and filed an affidavit to the effect that the matter
has been settled between the parties and she has no
objection to the proceedings in the criminal case being

quashed. Having regard to the nature of the case I am

(CrLM.C.No.7411/2014.

satisfied that this is a fit case to invoke the jurisdiction of
this Court under Section 482 Cr.PC.

3. In the result, this petition is allowed. The
proceedings in CC.No.485/2012 on the file of the Judicial

Magistrate of the First Class, Malappuram are quashed.
