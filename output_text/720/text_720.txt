ORDER

Petitioners are accused Nos.1 to 5 in C.C.No.776 of
2017 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court,
Malappuram, which has arisen from Crime No.343 of 2016 of
the Vengara Police Station, registered for the offences
punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 341, 323, 324,

294(b) and 506(i) IPC read with Section 149 IPC.

2. It'seems that the matter has been amicably settled
between the parties.

3. On going through the facts and circumstances of
the case, it seems that the matter can be permitted to be
settled. It has been reported that no criminal antecedents
have been reported against the petitioners. The defacto
complainant and the other affected person have filed
Annexure-A2 and Annexure-A3 affidavits affirming that the
matter has been amicably settled between them and the

petitioners and they have no complaints against the
 CrLM.C.No.8661 of 2017 2

petitioners. Considering the fact that the matter has already
been settled amicably between the parties, this court is
satisfied that no purpose would be served in proceeding with
the trial of the case. Matters being so, Annexure-Al Final
Report and all further proceedings in C.C.No.776 of 2017 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court, Malappuram,
which has arisen from Crime No.343 of 2016 of the Vengara
Police Station, as against the petitioners, are liable to be
quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-Al
Final Report and all further proceedings in C.C.No.776 of
2017 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court,
Malappuram, which has arisen from Crime No.343 of 2016 of

the Vengara Police Station, as against the petitioners, are

hereby quashed.
