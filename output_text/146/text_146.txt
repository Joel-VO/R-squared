ORDER

1.The petitioner is the sole accused in C.C.No.143 of 2013
on the file of the Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate Court
(EO), Ernakulam. On his failure to appear before the
learned Magistrate, non bailable warrant has been issued
against him.

2.In this petition filed under Section 482 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure, the prayer is to issue necessary
directions to the learned Magistrate to consider his
application for recall of warrant and grant of bail on the
date of surrender itself. According to the petitioner, the
petitioner was studying outside the state of Kerala and
there are no wilful latches.

3.Having heard the learned counsel and the learned Public

Prosecutor, this petition is disposed of by ordering that if
