ORDER

Petitioner challenges Annexure il order in Crl.A No.448/2023 on the

files of the Additional Sessions Court, North Paravur. As per the

impugned order, the sentence imposed upon the petitioner in
S.T.No.570/2016 was suspended on condition that the accused executes
a bond for Rs.50,000/- and on a further condition that he shall deposit a
sum of Rs.25,000/-.

2. Sri,U.K.Devidas, learned Counsel for the petitioner submitted
that the total cheque amount involved in the criminal proceedings was
only Rs.39,411/- and the appellate Court has imposed a condition to
deposit a sum of Rs.25,000/- without any reason.

3. Considering the nature of contentions advanced and the
nature of order that | propose to issue, notice to the 1* respondent is
disposed with.

4, It has already been held in Jamboo Bhandari v. M.P.
State Industrial Development Corporation Ltd. [2023 (6) KHC 80)
that the reasons for imposing condition must be stipulated. Since in the

impugned order no reasons have been given by the learned Sessions
