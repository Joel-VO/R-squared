LUDGMENT
1. This is a case charge sheeted by the Sub Inspector of Police, Poojappura Police

Station Crime No, 1411/2024 against the accused alleging commission of offences
ws 303(2) of BNS.

2. The case of the prosecution as revealed from the final report is that on 15/11/2024 at

sometime between 8 am and 8.40 am, the accused committed theft of Honda Activa
 seooter bearing registration No. KL-21-N-ISI1 which was parked in the
premises of defacto complainant's house at Kairali Nagar in Thirumala Village
‘nd thereby committed the offence as alleged against him.

Accused produced. He is under custody from 20/11/2024. Legal Aid Defence

Counsel is present. Accused filed petition to plead guilty. Hence, charge for

the offences w/s 303(2) of BNS was framed, read over and explained to the
accused to which he pleaded guilty. The plea is voluntarily made, with proper
legal assistance and after fully understanding the consequences thereof. The
accused is found guilty of the offence w's 303(2) of BNS and is convicted
thereunder. Heard the accused on the question of sentence w/s 271(2) of
BNSS.

Accused pleaded for leniency and prayed for set off for the period under
custody. Accused is sentenced to undergo Rigorous Imprisonment for a period
of 8 months for the offence ws 303(2) of BNS. Set off allowed wef
20/11/2024.

Property produced in this case as T 441/2024 Honda Activa bearing
registration No. KL-21-N-1511 and key released on interim custody shall
