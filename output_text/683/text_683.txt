ORDER
Dated this the 8th day of January, 2020

The petitioners are accused in Crime No.403/2019 of
Chittoor Police Station, Palakkad for having allegedly committed
offences punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 341, 323, 324 and
308 read with Section 34 of the IPC. The defacto complainant and
injured are respondents 2 to 5. They have appeared through counsel

and filed affidavits to the effect that the matter has been amicably

settled and that they do not have any grievance against the petitioners.
‘There is also a counter case registered against the respondents herein
as Crime No.402/2019 of the same Police Station which also has been
settled. A report has been obtained from the Investigating Officer
regarding the settlement. The petitioners do not have any criminal

antecedents. There is no public interest involved.

In the result, the CriM.C is allowed and the entire
proceedings in Crime No.403/2019 of Chittoor Police Station stands

quashed under Section 482 Cr.P.C and the accused are discharged.
