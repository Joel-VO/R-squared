ORDER

Petitioner is the 44” accused in Crime No.42/1992 of
Oachira Police Station pending as L.P.No.144/2012 on the file of
Judicial First Class Magistrate Court, Karunagappally. The
offences alleged are punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148,
324, 332, 333, 307, 120(b) read with Section 149 of IPC and
Section 3(2)(a) of Prevention of Destruction of Public Properties
Act.

2. Originally mob violence was registered on 01.04.1992
against 400 persons and final report was filed against 101
persons. 70 of the accused faced trial in S.C.Nos.216/1995,

8/1996 and 25/1997 before the AsstSessions Court, Kollam.

The trial court acquitted all the accused persons 1g that
the injured police men failed to identify any of the accused.
The trial court found that there is no evidence against the
accused regarding criminal conspiracy and their involvement in
the violence and all of them were acquitted. A copy of the
judgment dt.08.10.2002 is produced as Annexure 3.
Proceedings against other six accused were quashed by this
Court through three separate judgments. Annexures 4, 5 and

6 are the copies of the said judgments.
