ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in CC No.630/2014 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-l, Vadakara,
which has arisen from Crime No.1552/2013 of Badakara
Police Station for the offences punishable under Sections
143, 147, 148, 323, 324, 294(b) and 506(ii) read with
Section 149 IPC.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the petitioners and the de facto
‘complainant, who is the 2 respondent herein.

3. The 2 respondent has filed Annexure-B affidavit
affirming that the matter has been amicably settled between
him and the petitioners and presently, he has no complaints
against the petitioners. No purpose would be served in

proceeding with the matter further. Matters being so, all
 (CrL.M.C.8883/2017

further proceedings against. the petitioners in CC
No.630/2014 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court,
Vadakara, which has arisen from Crime No.1552/2013 of
Badakara Police Station, can be quashed.

In the result, this Cr.M.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings against the petitioners in CC No.630/2014 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-l, Vadakara,
which has arisen from Crime No.1552/2013 of Badakara
Police Station, are quashed
