ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in Crime No.1218
of 2016 of Peringome Police Station registered
under Section 174 Cr.P.c. Third respondent is
the informant. In the course of the
investigation, the investigation officer reported
that the investigation revealed commission of the
offences under Sections 201, 306, 406 and 420
IPC, The accused allegedly abetted suicide of
the father-in-law of the third respondent. It is
submitted that the matter has been settled. The

prayer is to quash the proceedings.

2. Heard the learned counsel for the
petitioners and the third respondent and the

learned Public Prosecutor.

3. I have perused the affidavit filed by the
third respondent who has entered appearance

through counsel. I am satisfied that the matter
 Cel.MC.No. 4960 of 2018 4 2019KERDS

has been settled. In Annexure AV Order this
court took the view that the facts of the case do
not attract the offences under Section 306 IPC.
No public interest is involved in this case.
There is no impediment for quashing the

proceedings

In the result, this Crl.M.c. is allowed. The
proceedings in Crime No.1218 of 2016 of Peringome
Police Station are quashed. The learned
Magistrate is directed to dispose of material

objects if any produced in this case.
