LUDGMENT
1. Accused stands trial for offences punishable under sections 341, 323 and 324

read with section 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2, The prosecution case in_brief is_as follows :- On 21.11.2012, at 9.00
house,Nirmalalayam Convent Thekkekunnu road Manvila, Kulathoor Ward,
Attipra Village, accused No.1 wrongfully restrained and hit on his chest and
shoulder and he again beat on his chest and kicked on his abdomen. Further
accused No.1 beat CW2 on his shoulder with a chair. Accused No.1 beaten
CWI on his with a wooden piece when he evaded the same with his left hand
thereby caused caused injury and caused dislocation of his hand. Further
accused uttered obscene words and threatened to kill him. Thus accused Nos. 1
and 2 thereby committed offenses w’s. 341, 323 and 324 read with section 34 of

Indian Penal Code, 1860.
