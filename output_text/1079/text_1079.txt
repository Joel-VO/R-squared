This case having been heard on today the court on the same day passed the

following:
ORDER
1. Accused stands charged with the offences punishable w/s. 55(i) of the Kerala
‘Abkari Act.

2. Prosecution case in brief is as follows:- On 031.08.2023, at 08.15 hours, at
Muttathara Sajeev Nagar ~ PKP road, Muttathara Desam, Muttathara Village,
‘Thiruvananthapuram Taluk, accused was found in possession of 5.5 litres of
Indian Made Foreign Liquor for the purpose of sale, in contravention of the
provisions of the Kerala Abkari Act. Thereby the accused committed above
mentioned offence.

3. On filing final report, the case was taken on file as CP No. 70/2024, When
process served, the accused person entered appearance. He was enlarged on
bail during crime stage and allowed to continue in the same. Copies of all

prosecution records were furnished him as provided under section 207 of
CrPC.
 2082

4. On perusal of the case records, it appears to me that the offences alleged against
the accused is exclusively triable by the Hon’ble Sessions Court. Hence, the
case is to be committed to the Hon’ble Sessions Court, Thiruvananthapuram.
Therefore, the case is hereby committed to the Hon’ble Sessions Court,
‘Thiruvananthapuram w/s.209 of Cr.P.C.

5. Issue notice to the Public Prosecutor. Notify him of the committal of the case
and transmit records and material objects to the Hon’ble Sessions Court as per
Rules.

6. The accused shall appear before the Hon'ble Court of Sessions,
‘Thiruvananthapuram on issuance of summons.
