ORDER

The Court of the Judicial Magistrate of the first class - II,
Kollam has issued Annexure - B notice informing the petitioner
that the Chief Judicial Magistrate, Kollam has authorized that court

to record the statement of the petitioner under Section 164 of the

Code of Criminal Procedure in connection with Crime No.776 of

2019 of Anchalummoodu Police Station and directing the
petitioner to appear before the court for the said purpose on
25.11.2019. Annexure - B notice is under challenge in this
CrLM.C.

also the learned Public Prosecutor.

statement is necessary under Section 164 of the Code of Criminal
Procedure, she is prepared to appear before any Magistrate in
Malappuram district, if not inconvenient, before the Judicial
Magistrate of the First Class Nilambur, for the said purpose.
