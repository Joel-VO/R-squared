3.The prosecution allegation is that on 06/02/2025 at 10:15 am, the accused was

autorickshaw, for the purpose of sale in contravention of the provisions of the Abkari

‘Act. Thus, the accused is said to have committed the above offences.

4, Heard both sides.
 2
5. The learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the petitioner was arrested

‘on 06/02/2025 and he has been in judicial custody. The counsel appeared for the
Petitioner further submitted that the petitioner is innocent and he is falsely implicated in

6. On the other hand, the leamed Additional Public Prosecutor opposed the bail
application,

‘can be released on bail by imposing conditions.
