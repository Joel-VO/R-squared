ORDER

1. This is a case wherein Charge Sheet was filed by the Sub Inspector of
Police, Sreekaryam Police Station in Crime No. 809/24 against the Accused
alleging commission of offences punishable u/Ss.1109(1), 118(2) of BNS and 75 JJ
Act.

2. The Prosee

case is that the Petitioner herein due to some unknown

animosity towards his wife and 10-year-old child, stabbed them both in their
abdomen with a knife on 06.08.2024 at 09:00 AM in the morning in their rented

idence. The wife and minor child of the Petitioner were seriously injured and

taken to the hospital in critical care. Thereby the Petitioner is alleged to have
committed the aforesaid offences.
 3. Accused is under custody. Copies of all relevant Prosecution records were

furnished to him and thereby the mandate u/s. 207 of Cr-P.C. was complied

with.

4. From the perusal of the records, it is noted that the offences alleged

against the Accused, those which are exclusively triable by the Hon'ble Court of

Sessions as it involves offence under section Juvenile Justice Care and Protection

of Children Act, 2015. Hence, this case is therefore committed to the Hon'ble

Court of Sessions, Thiruvananthapuram under section U/s.20%a) Cr.PC.

5. The Accused continues to remain in judicial custody. He shall be

produced before the Hon'ble Court of Sessions,Thiruvananthapuram as and

when required.

be forwarded to the Honâ€™ble Court of Sessions, Thiruvananthapuram at the

earliest, subject to Rule 77(2) of the Criminal Rules of Practice, Kerala.

7. Notify the Learned Assistant Public Prosecutor as to the committal of the

above case to the Hon'ble Court of Sessions U/s.209(d) of Code of Criminal

Procedure, 1973.

8. The office is directed to keep copies of all relevant records prior to the

same being send to the Hon'ble Court of Sessions.
