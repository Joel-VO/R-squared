ORDER
Dated this the 7th day of January 2020

Petitioners are accused 1 and 8 in Crime No.121/2015,
of Karuvarakundu Police Station for having allegedly
committed offences punishable under Sections 143, 147,
148, 341, 323 and 324 r/w Section 149 of IPC along with
several other accused. Petitioners are absconding and the
case against them was split up and refiled as C.C.
No.1068/2017 on the files of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate Court-I, Manjeri. The co-accused who faced trial
were found not guilty and acquitted vide judgment dated
31.10.2017 at Annexure A2 and the petitioners are
presently facing trial.

2. There is only one injured, who is the 3° respondent.
He is appeared through counsel and filed affidavit to the
effect that the matter has been amicably settled and that he
has no grievance whatsoever against the petitioners. A
report has been called for from the investigating officer
through the Senior Public Prosecutor. The petitioners do not

have criminal antecedents. There is no public interest
 CrL.MC.No.8253 OF 2019(B) 3 2n20iKeRiso6
involved. All the co-accused have been acquitted and that
acquittal has become final.

In the result, the Cri.M.C is allowed and the entire
proceedings in CC No.1068/2017 on the files of the Judicial
First Class Magistrate Court-I, Manjeri stands quashed under

Section 482 of Cr.P.C.and the accused are discharged.
