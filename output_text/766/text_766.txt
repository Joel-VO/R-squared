ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in CC No.1186/2017
of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-ll,
Pathanamthitta, which has arisen from Crime No.1301/2017
of Konni Police Station for the offences punishable under
Sections 294(b), 323 and 324 read with Section 34 IPC.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the petitioners and the de facto
complainant, who is the 2” respondent herein. The 3
respondent is the other injured.

3. Respondents 2 and 3 have filed Annexures-A3
and A4 affidavits, affirming that the matter has been
amicably settled between them and the petitioners and
presently, they have no complaints against the petitioners.

No purpose would be served in proceeding with the matter
 (CrLM.C.8860/2017

further. Matters being so, all further proceedings against the
petitioners in CC No.1186/2017 of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate's Court-ll, Pathanamthitta, which has arisen from
Crime No.1301/2017 of Konni Police Station, can be
quashed

In the result, this CrlM.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings against the petitioners in CC No.1186/2017 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Courtll,
Pathanamthitta, which has arisen from Crime No.1301/2017

of Konni Police Station, are quashed.
