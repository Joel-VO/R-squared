ORDER
The petitioners are accused in CC.No.1833/2014 on
the file of the Judicial Magistrate of the First Class-I,
Thrissur. They are alleged to have committed the offence
under Section 498A IPC. It is submitted that the matter has
been settled between the parties and it is prayed that the
proceedings in the criminal case may be quashed.
2. Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and
of the first respondent and the learned Public Prosecutor.
3. The first petitioner is the husband of the first
respondent and the other petitioners are his parents. The

first respondent has entered appearance through a counsel

and filed an affidavit to the effect that the matter has been
settled between the parties. I have perused the allegations
in the final report and the averments in the affidavit filed by

the first respondent, who has entered appearance through a

is a fit case to quash the proceedings in the criminal case.

4. In the result, this petition is allowed. The

Magistrate of the First Class-1, Thrissur are quashed.
