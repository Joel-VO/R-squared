ORDER

‘The petitioner is the accused in C.C.No.1039/2013 of the
Judicial First Class Magistrate Court 1, Mavelikkara for offences
punishable under Sections 452, 427, 323, 324 and 294(b) of IPC.

2. According to the prosecution, on 28.08.2013 at 5.30
p.m., the accused came to the shop of the defacto complainant,
abused her and tried to assault the defacto complainant. When
her husband intervened, he slapped on the cheek of the husband
of the defacto complainant and when the wife intervened, he hit
‘on her cheek as well as on various other parts. He also abused
and assaulted the mentally challenged son of the defacto
complainant. On the basis of the complaint lodged, crime was
registered. After investigation final report was laid and the matter
is now pending as C.C.No.1039/2013 of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate Court |, Mavelikkara.

3. The petitioner has approached this Court on a premise
that, the disputes between parties have now been voluntarily and
amicably settled. it was stated that, there is no further surviving

grievance of the defacto complainant. To substantiate it,
