ORDER

Heard the learned counsel for the petitioners and respondents.

2. Accused in Crime No.184/2013 of Bekal Police Station
Pending as C.C.No.1160/2013 on the file of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate Court II, Hosdurg are the petitioners. The petition is filed
seeking to quash further proceedings against the petitioners in
‘Annexure- All Final Report in Crime No. 184/2013 of Bekal Police
Station.

3. The offences alleged are punishable under Sections 498 A of
IPC.

4, The learned counsel on both sides submit that the disputes
between the parties have been amicably settled and having regard to
the resolution of the disputes between the parties, continuance of the
criminal prosecution against the petitioners is an avoidable irritant.

In view of the settlement arrived at between the parties, the
CrLM.C is disposed of quashing further proceedings against the
petitioners in Annexure All Final Report in Crime No. 184/2013 of

Bekal Police Station.
