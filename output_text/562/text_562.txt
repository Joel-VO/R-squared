ORDER

Petitioner is the de facto complainant and
respondents 2 to 6 are the accused in Crime No.348/2015
of Koduvally Police Station, registered for the offences
punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 341, 427, 307
and 395 read with Section 149 IPC.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the petitioner, who is the de facto
complainant and respondents 2 to 6, who are the accused,

3. Petitioner, who is the de facto complainant, has
filed Annexure-A2 affidavit affirming that the matter has
been amicably settled between him and the accused and
presently, he has no complaints against the
accused/respondents 2 to 6. When the matter has been

amicably settled between the parties, no purpose would be
 (CrLM.C.6729/2017

served in proceeding with the matter any further. Matters
being so, Annexure-A1 First Information Report in Crime
No.348/2015 of Koduvally Police Station and all further
proceedings as against respondents 2 to 6 in it, can be
quashed

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-A1
First Information Report in Crime No.348/2015 of Koduvally
Police Station and all further proceedings as against

respondents 2 to 6 in it, are quashed.
