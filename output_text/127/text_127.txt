Petition filed under Section 482 Cr.P.C.

2. Petitioners are the accused in
C.C.No.127/2013 on the file of the Judicial
Magistrate of First Class-II, Nedumangad. They

has been settled and the proceedings in the

the matter has been settled and no public
interest is involved in this case, ‘There is no
impediment for granting the prayer of the

petitioners.
