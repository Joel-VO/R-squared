Petitioners are the accused in CC No.17/2016 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-IX, Ernakulam,
which has arisen from Crime No.835/2008 of Palarivattom
Police Station for the offences punishable under Sections
341, 323 and 294(b) read with Section 34 IPC

settled between the petitioners and the de facto
‘complainant, who is the 2“ respondent herein.

affidavit affirming that the matter has been amicably settled
between him and the petitioners and presently, he has no
complaints against the petitioners. No purpose would be
served in proceeding with the matter further. Matters being

so, all further proceedings against the petitioners in CC
