This Cri.M.C. under Section 482 of Cr.P.C., is filed by
the sole accused in Crime No.130 of 2024 of Angamali
Police Station, Ernakulam, registered under Sections 354

and 354A of IPC, for quashing Annexure- A1 FIR.

2. The prosecution allegation is that on
20.11.2023, at about 3 p.m., the accused, who is a doctor
in Apollo Hospital, Karukutty, Ernakulam District, tried to
sexually abuse the 1 respondent in the treatment room of

that hospital.

learned counsel for the petitioner submitted that the

respondent/defacto complainant. The defacto complainant

entered appearance through counsel, and submitted that
