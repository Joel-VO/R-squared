ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in CC No.498/2017 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court, Taiparamba,
which has arisen from Crime No.1183/2016 of Payyavoor
Police Station for the offences punishable under Sections
143, 147, 148, 452, 341, 323, 324 and 506(ji) read with
Section 149 IPC

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the petitioners and the de facto
complainant, who is the 2” respondent herein. The 3”
respondent is the other injured.

3. Respondents 2 and 3 have filed separate
affidavits, affirming that the matter has been amicably
settled between them and the petitioners and presently, they

have no complaints against the petitioners. No purpose
 (CrL.M.C.8201/2017

would be served in proceeding with the matter further.
Matters being so, all further proceedings against the
petitioners in CC No.498/2017 of the Judicial First Class
Magistrate's Court, Taliparamba, which has arisen from
Crime No,1183/2016 of Payyavoor Police Station, can be
quashed

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings against the petitioners in CC No.498/2017 of
the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court, Taiparamba,
which has arisen from Crime No.1183/2016 of Payyavoor

Police Station, are quashed.
