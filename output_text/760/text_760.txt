ORDER
Petitioners are accused Nos.1 to 6 in Crime No.531 of
2017 of the Mannarkkad Police Station, registered for the
offences punishable under Sections 143, 147, 341 and 323
IPC read with Section 149 IPC.

2. Itseems that the matter has been amicably settled
between the parties.

3. On going through the facts and circumstances of
the case, it seems that the matter can be permitted to be
settled. It has been reported that no criminal antecedents
have been reported against the petitioners. The defacto
complainant and the other affected person have filed
Annexure-II and Annexure-Iil affidavits affirming that the
matter has been amicably settled between them and the
petitioners and they have no complaints against the
petitioners. Considering the fact that the matter has already

been settled amicably between the parties, this court is
 2018KERA7
(CrLM.C.No.8844 of 2017 2

satisfied that no purpose would be served in proceeding with
the trial of the case. Matters being so, Annexure-| First
Information Report in Crime No.531 of 2017 of the
Mannarkkad Police Station, and all further proceedings in it,
as against the petitioners, are liable to be quashed.

In the result, this Cr.M.C. is allowed and Annexure-|
First Information Report in Crime No.531 of 2017 of the
Mannarkkad Police Station, and all further proceedings in it,

as against the petitioners, are hereby quashed.
