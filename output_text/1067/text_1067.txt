This case having been heard on today the court on the same day passed the

ORDER

1. Accused stands charged with the offences punishable wss. 294 (b), 447, 323,
341, 506(ii), 308, 427 and 201 read with section 34 of Indian Penal Code,
1860.

2. Prosecution case in brief is as follows:-. Due to the enmity that CW1 asked
the accused persons to pay for the tea, on 11.06.2024, at 16.30 hours, the
accused no. 1 and 2 trespassed into the tea shop own by CW1 and fisted on
right eye, when he evaded the same, it fell on his forehead, thereafter accused
no.1 left there. It is alleged that on the same day night at 17.30 hours, accused
no.1 along with accused No.2 came to the spot and again trespassed into the
 2082

tea shop, accused no.1 among them by uttering obscene words, slapped on his
cheek and stabbed him using a knife. Further, the accused destroyed the glass
shelf, keeping food articles and the the vessels kept there and thereby caused
loss of Rs. 1,00,000/- to CW1. Thus the accused committed an offence
punishable u/ss. 294 (b), 447, 323, 341, 506(ii), 308, 427 and 201 read with
section 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. On filing final report, the case was taken on file as CP No. 34/2024. When
process served, the accused No.1 entered appearance. He was enlarged on bail
during crime stage and allowed to continue in the same. Copies of all

prosecution records were furnished him as provided under section 230 of
Bharatiya Nagatik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023. Case against accused No.2 is split up
and refiled as CP. 10/2025.

4, On perusal of the case records, it appears to me that the offences alleged against

the accused No.1 is exclusively triable by the Hon’ble Sessions Court. Hence,
the case is to be committed to the Hon’ble Sessions Court, Thiruvananthapuram.
Therefore, the case is hereby committed to the Hon’ble Sessions Court,
‘Thiruvananthapuram w/s.232 of Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023.

5. Issue notice to the Public Prosecutor. Notify him of the committal of the case
and transmit records and material objects produced as T No, 256/2024 to the
Hon'ble Sessions Court as per Rules.

6. The accused No.1 shall appear before the Hon’ble Court of Sessions,
‘Thiruvananthapuram on issuance of summons.
