JUDGMENT
1. Accused stands trial for offences punishable under sections 294(b), 323 and

324 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2. ‘The prosecution case in brief is as follows ;- Due to the prior enmity on
19.08.2023, at 20.30 hours, the accused uttered obscene words against CW1 at
Cheriyamuttam — Beach Lane, Poonthura Ward, Muttathara Village.
‘Thereafter the accused struck on his forehead and both knees with a jacky
lever, resulting in injury. Thus accused commited offences punishable under

sections 294(b), 323 and 324 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. Final report was filed before this court by the Sub Inspector of police
Poonthura police station and the case was taken on file as CC.551/2021.
Cognizance was taken for the offences punishable w/ss. 294(b), 323 and 324 of

Indian Penal Code, 1860 against the accused.
 3o0fS
4. On appearance of the accused, he was enlarged on bail on 21.08.2021. Copies

of all relevant prosecution records were furnished to him under section 207
Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973. After hearing prosecution and counsel for
accused charge framed for offences w/ss. 294(b), 323 and 324 of Indian Penal
Code, 1860, read over and explained to the accused to which he pleaded not

guilty and claimed to be tried.

5. On the

le of prosecution, PWI was examined and Ext. Pl was marked. As
the material witnesses failed to support the prosecution case, the learned
Assistant Public Prosecutor gave up the CW2 to CW6. In the absence of any
incriminating circumstances against the accused questioning of the accused
‘under section 313(1)(b) Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 was dispensed with.
Accused was called upon to adduce evidence but no defence evidence was

adduced from the side of the accused.

6. Heard both sides and perued records.

8. Boint Nos. 1 to 3 :-To avoid repetition and for brevity, point numbers 1 to 3
are considered together. Prosecution case is that accused has committed
offences punishable under sections 294(b), 323 and 324 of Indian Penal Code,
1860,

9. PW1 admitted his signature in the Ext. PI statement. According to PW1, the

accused has assaulted him and he has no grievance against the accused.

10, The learned Assistant Public Prosecutor had given up CW2 to CW6 and she is
Justified in doing so as the material witness did not support the case of
prosecution. Having considered evidence on record, this court is of the view
that prosecution has failed to prove the guilt of accused. For the said reason

these points are found against the prosecution.
