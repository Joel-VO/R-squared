ORDER

The petitioners are accused in S.C.464/2015 on the
files of the court below. The offences alleged are punishable
under sections 143, 147, 148, 341, 323, 324 and 427 read
with Section 149 of IPC.

2. The respondent Nos. 2 to 4 are the injured in the
above case. The respondent Nos. 2 to 4 have filed separate
affidavit stating that the matter has been settled by the
parties and hence they have no further grievance against
the petitioners.

3. Considering the nature of allegations, I am of the
view that the settlement of the dispute will lead to peace
and harmony in the locality. That apart, since the matter
has been settled, there is no chance for culminating the

trial in conviction.
 2019%KERIS

CrLMC.No. 8813 of 2018

In view of the above reasons, I am of the view that it
is only just and proper to quash the final report and further
proceedings against the petitioners in S.C.No.464/2015 on
the files of the court below in exercise of the inherent
power under Section 482 of CrP.C. to meet the ends of
justice. It is ordered accordingly.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. stands allowed as above.
