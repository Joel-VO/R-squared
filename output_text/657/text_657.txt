ORDER

Petitioner is the accused in L.P.No.40 of 2016 of the
Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate's Court (Economic
Offences), Ernakulam, which is arising out of Crime No.2020 of
2011 of E.T.South Police Station, registered for the offences
punishable under Sections 283, 323, 294 (b) of the Indian
Penal Code.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably settled
between the parties.

3. On going through the facts and circumstances of the
case, it seems that the matter can be permitted to be settled.

4, It has been reported that no criminal antecedents
have been reported against the petitioner. The second
respondent has filed Annexure-II affidavit affirming that the
matter has been amicably settled between him and the

petitioner and he has no complaints against the petitioner.
 2017KERIIS66

Considering the fact that the matter has already been settled
amicably between the parties, this court is satisfied that no
Purpose would be served in proceeding with the trial of the
case, Matters being so, all further proceedings in L.P.No.40 of
2016 of the Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate's Court
(Economic Offences), Ernakulam, which is arising out of Crime
No.2020 of 2011 of E.T.South Police Station, against the
petitioner, are liable to be quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and all further
Proceedings in L.P.No.40 of 2016 of the Additional Chief
Judicial Magistrate's Court (Economic Offences), Ernakulam,
which is arising out of Crime No.2020 of 2011 of E.T.South

Police Station, against the petitioner, are hereby quashed.
