ORDER

The prayer in this Cri.M.C is to quash the proceedings in
C.C.No.265 of 2014 on the file of JFCM, Nedumkandam. Being a
CrILM.C of the year 2015, pertaining to a crime of 2014, a report was
called for, from the jurisdictional Magistrate Court. In the report, the
learned Magistrate has reported that as per judgment dated
18.08.2017 in C.C.No.265 of 2014, the accused was acquitted under
Section 248(1) Cr.P.C. In the light of the said report, the Cri.M.C is
closed.
