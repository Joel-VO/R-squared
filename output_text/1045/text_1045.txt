2. Prosecution case in_brief isas follows:- In furtherance of previous animosity
‘towards CWI accused No.1 along with accused No. 1 to 5, on 02.06.2024, at 01.15 pm
reached at House No. 141, Erappukuzhi SRO, Pathirappally Ward, where the
informant and his family resides, in two motorcycles. Accused Nos. 1 to 5 trespassed
into the house of CW1, Binu Bhaskar with an intention to commit culpable homicide.
Accused Nos. 1 to 5 broken the glasses of the car bearing registration No. KL 01 BQ
13 stalled at the car shed by pelting stones. Accused No.2 by using a chopper
destroyed the petrol tank of motor cycle bearing registration No, KL. 01 BX 1091
stalled CW1 of the house and he again chopped at the gate, ‘Thereafter, accused pelted
stones at the wife, mother and children of CW1. Thus accused Nos. 1 to 5 committed
‘offences punishable ws. 143, 147, 149, 294(b), 324, 308, 452, 427, 201 and 506(ii) of
Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. On filing final report, the case was taken on file as CP No. 01/2025. When process
served, the accused Nos. 1, 3,4 and 5 entered appearance. ‘They were enlarged on bail
during crime stage and allowed to continue in the same. Copies of all prosecution
records were furnished them as provided under section 230 of Bharatiya Nagarik
Suraksha Sanhita, 2023. Case against accused No.2 is split up and refiled as CP,
1672025.

4, On perusal of the case records, it appears to me that the offences alleged against the
accused Nos. 1, 3, 4 and 5 are exclusively triable by the Hon'ble Sessions Court
Hence, the case is to be committed to the Hon'ble Sessions Court,
‘Thiruvananthapuram. ‘Therefore, the case is hereby committed to the Hon'ble
Sessions Court, Thiravananthapuram w/s.232 of Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita,
2023.
