JUDGMENT.

1. This is a case instituted upon the Final report filed by Sub Inspector of
Police, City Traffic Police Station in Crime No.3338/2013 for the offences
punishable under Sections 279, 337 and 304 A of the Indian Penal Code, 1860
(hereinafter referred to as ‘the IPC’).

2. The Prosecution allegation in brief is as follows: Accused was the driver of
the Autorickshaw bearing Registration No. Kl 01 BA 9510. He drove the vehicle
from Sasthamangalam to Vellayambalam on Sasthamangalam-Vellayambalam Roa,
rashly, negligently and in a manner so as to endanger human life and at high speed
about on 04.06.2023 at 09:30 PM, The right side of car hit on the front right side of
the motor cycle bearing Registration No. KL 02 Temp 851, driven by the since
deceased in the same direction along with the Informant as its pillion rider, when
‘the car immediately and in high-speed drove towards the right side and took an
immediate U-turn, on the road in front of Indira Bhavan, Due to its impact,
Informant sustained injuries on his head, right leg and right hand and the since
deceased sustained grievous injuries on his head and legs. He was initially taken to
KIMS hospital and later to Medical College Hospital, Thiruvananthapuram.
‘Thereafter, on 29.06.2013 at 9.45 pm, the since Deceased succumbed to his
injuries, while undergoing treatment. Thus, the Accused is alleged to have
‘committed the above stated offences.

3. This Court took cognizance in respect of offences under Sections 279, 337
‘and 304 A of the IPC, On issuance of process, the Accused entered appearance. He
was enlarged on bail. He was represented by a Counsel of his own choice. Copies
of all relevant prosecution records were furnished to him. The particulars of
offence under Sections 279, 337 and 304 A of the IPC were read over and
‘explained to him, to which he pleaded not guilty and claimed to be tried.

4, From the side of Prosecution, CWs, 3, 1, 5, 17, 2 and 16 were examined as
PWsI to 6 respectively and Exhibits P1 to P12 were marked, amongst which
Exhibits P9 to 11 were marked subject to Proof. CWG is no more and his death
certificate was produced and CW7 did not turn up before the Court despite having
effected steps under Section 82 of the Code of Criminal Procedure (hereinafter
referred to as the CrPC). The remaining witnesses (CWs4, 8 to 15) were given up
by the Learned APP. The Accused was examined Section 313(1)(b) of the CrPC and
he denied all the incriminating circumstances against him and claimed innocencs
No Defence evidence was adduced.

arise for consideration.

manner endangering the life of since deceased and PW2, causing injury to
them and thereby committed the offence punishable under Section 279 of
the IPC as alleged?

b. Has the Accused driven the aforesaid vehicle rashly, negligently in a
‘manner endangering the life of since deceased and PW2, causing hurt to
them and thereby committed the offence punishable under Section 279 of
the IPC as alleged?

c. Has the Accused by so driving the aforesaid vehicle in rash or

Further, the contradictions in the deposition of the PWS is not proved as and the
