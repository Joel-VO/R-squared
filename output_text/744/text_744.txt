Dated this the 06" day of January, 2020

The petitioners are accused in C.C.No. 173/2019 on
the files of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court Il, Kochi for
having allegedly committed offences punishable under Sections
341, 323, 324 and 294(b) read with Section 34 of the IPC. The
defacto complainant and injured are respondents 2 to 4 who
have appeared through counsel and filed affidavits to the effect
that the matter has been amicably settled with the petitioners
and that they have no grievance against them. A report has
been submitted by the Investigating Officer through the learned
Senior Public Prosecutor to the said effect. The petitioners do

not have any criminal antecedents.

In the result, the Crl.MC is allowed and the entire
proceedings as against the petitioners in C.C.No.173/2019 on
the files of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court Il, Kochi
stands quashed under Section 482 of the Cr.C and the accused

are discharged
