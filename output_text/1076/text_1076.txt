JUDGMENT

1. Accused stands trial for offences punishable under sections 294(b) and 323

read with section 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2. Prosecution _case_in_brief_is_as_follows:- Due to the prior enmity, on
17.10.2018, at 18.30 hours, the accused no.1 by uttering obscene words, fisted
fon the cheek of CW1 at the Kallayam Junction, Plavuvila Ward, Vattappara
Village, and by kicking on his abdomen, pushed him towards the public road,
the accused no.2 caught his head and hit it against the road resulting injuries.
It's alleged that accused nos. 1 and 2 beaten all over the body of CWI. Thus
the accused has committed offences punishable w/ss, 294(b) and 323 read with

section 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. Final report was filed before this court by the Sub Inspector of police
‘Mannanthala police station and the case was taken on file as CC. 2647/2018.
Cognizance was taken for the offences punishable w/s 294(b) and 323 read with

section 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860 against the accused Nos. 1 and 2.

10, Learned Assistant Public Prosecutor has given up the other witnesses in this

committed the offences as alleged by the prosecution. Having considered the
 5.016
