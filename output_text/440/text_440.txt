ORDER

1.The 1* petitioner is the owner of a lorry bearing
registration No.KL-10-V-6473. The aforesaid vehicle was
seized on 28.7.2016 on the allegation that the same was
used for illegal transportation of ordinary earth.

2.As per Rule 32 of the Kerala

erals (Prevention of illegal
Mining, Storage and Transportation) Rules, 2015
(hereinafter referred to as “the Transportation
Rules,2015") any offence punishable under the said rules
can be compounded, either before or after the institution
of the prosecution, by the person authorized under
Section 22 of the Mines and Minerals (Development and

Regulation) Act, 1957 or clause (ii) of Rule 2 of the
