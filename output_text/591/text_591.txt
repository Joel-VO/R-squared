THIS CRIMINAL MISC. CASE HAVING COME UP FOR ADMISSION ON
06.01.2020, THE COURT ON THE SAME DAY PASSED THE FOLLOWING:
 CrL.MC.No.7179 OF 2019(F) 3, 2n20KERD7

ORDER
Dated this the 6th day of January 2020

Petitioners are accused in Crime No.30/2018 of
Thottilpalam Police Station for having allegedly committed

offences punishable under Sections 323 and 324 r/w Set

34 IPC,

2, The de facto complainant are respondents 2,3 and 4.
The case against petitioners 1 to 3 was taken on file of the
Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-I, Nadapuram as C.C.
No.672/2018 and the case against the 4 petitioner, who was
then a Juvenile, is pending before the Juvenile Justice Board,
Kozhikode as S.T. No. 104/2018.

3.The de facto complainant and injured have appeared
through counsel and filed affidavits to the effect that the
matter has been amicably settled with the petitioners and that
they do not have any grievance against them. A report has
been called for from the investigating officer through the
Senior Public Prosecutor. Petitioners do not have criminal
antecedents. They were all students at the time of alleged

offence. No public interest is involved.
 Cr1.MC.No.7179 OF 2019(F) 4 2n20KERD7

In the result, the Cri.M.C. is allowed and the entire
proceedings in Crime No.30/2018 of Thottilpalam Police
Station presently pending against petitioners 1 to 3 as CC
No.672/2018 before the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court-I,
Nadapuram and against the 4" petitioner as ST No.104/2018
before the Juvenile Justice Board, Kozhikode stands quashed

under Section 482 of Cr-P.C and the accused are discharged.
