ORDER
Dated this the 06" day of January, 2020

The petitioner is the sole accused in CC.No.696/2017 on
the files of the Judicial first Class Magistrate Court, Chavakkad for
having allegedly committed offence punishable under Section 420
of the IPC. The defacto complainant and aggrieved are
respondents 2 to 7. They have appeared through counsel and filed
affidavits to the effect that the matter has been amicably settled
with the petitioner and that they do not have any grievance
against him. A report has been obtained from the Investing Officer
through the learned Senior Public Prosecutor. The petitioner does

not has any criminal antecedents.

In the result, the Cr.M.C is allowed and the entire
proceedings as against the petitioners in C.C.No.696/2017 on the
file of the Judicial First Class Magistrate Court, Chavakkad stands
quashed under Section 482 of the CrPRC and the accused is

discharged.
