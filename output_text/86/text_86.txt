ORDER.

Petitioner claims to be the registered owner of the vehicle
bearing registration No. KL-10-W-8603. The said vehicle was
seized by the respondent in connection with a crime of Edavanna
Police Station on the allegation of illegal transportation of river
sand.

2. The petitioner filed CMP No. 6155/2013 before the Judicial
First Class Magistrate Court-I, Manjeri seeking interim custody of
the vehicle. The court ordered interim custody subject to eight
conditions, The court ordered that the petitioner shall deposit
Rs.39,900/- and shall also furnish bank guarantee for Rs.
93,100/-. He is also directed to execute a bond for Rs. 1,33,000/-
with two solvent sureties each for the like sum.

3. The petitioner is aggrieved by the condition to deposit
Rs.39,900/- and to furnish bank guarantee for Rs.93,100/- as
security. It is submitted that he is unable to comply with the
‘above conditions and that he is willing to execute 2 bond for

Rs.1,33,000/- with two solvent sureties each for the like sum.
 2014KERDWO
CALM. No.792014

Having regard to the facts and circumstance of the case,
this Court is of the view that the conditions imposed by the court
below for the release of the vehicle shall be modified. The 3°
condition and the direction in the 4" condition to furnish bank
guarantee for Rs. 93,100/- stand deleted.

The Crl.M.C Is disposed of as above.
