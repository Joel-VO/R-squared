8, Boint Nos. 1 and 2 :-To avoid repetition and for brevity, point numbers 1 and
2 are considered together. Prosecution case is that accused Nos.1 and 2 have
committed offences punishable under sections 341 and 324 read with Sec. 34

of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

9. PWI deposed that he was an auto rickshaw driver and admitted his signature
in the Ext P1 statement . According to him on 3.5.2015 ,he was asked to
remove his auto rickshaw by two persons and they took away its key .One

among them hit on his shoulder,chest and head with a helmet resulting in

pain Being injured by the incident, he underwent treatment at General

Hospital, Thiruvananthapuram -He identified, the helmet, which was marked as
 S0f6
MO1. He further deposed that accused persons who were standing at the dock

never assaulted him and he has no grievance against them . It was also

deposed by him that he is not intending to proceed with the case.

10. The learned Assistant Public Prosecutor had given up other witnesses and he is
justified in doing so as the material witness did not support the case of
prosecution, Having considered evidence on record, this court is of the view
that prosecution has failed to prove the guilt of accused No. 1 for the said

reasons these points are found against the prosecution.

11, Point No.3 :- Upon the finding in point Nos. 1 and 2, accused No, 1 is found
not guilty of offences punishable under sections 341 and 324 read with Sec. 34
of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and he is acquitted of the said offences under
section 248(1) Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973 . His bail bond stand
cancelled and he is set at liberty, Case against accused No.2 is split up and
refiled as CC, 971/2024.
