ORDER

Petitioners were originally A27, A42, A48 in C.C.No.986
of 2008 of the Judicial First Class Magistrate's Court-II,
Hosdurg for the offences punishable under Sections 143, 147,
148, 452, 427, 506 (ii) and 153 (A) IPC r/w Section 149 IPC.

2. The incident was the result of a commotion occurred
between two groups. The case was the result of Annexure A2
final report in Crime No.404 of 2005 of the Bekal Police
Station.

3. 38 accused faced trial in C.C.No.986 of 2008. All
the said accused were acquitted for want of any evidence.
None of the accused were identified. There was no evidence in
the case, The case against the petitioners was split up and re-
filed. Subsequently, through Annexure-4 order, this Court had
quashed the case against A40 and A41. Over and above it,

through Ext.AS judgment, another accused who faced the trial
 2017KERIISO
CrLM.C. No.7835 of 2017

was also acquitted.

4. The case against the petitioners is pending as
C.C.No.924 of 2016 before the Judicial First Class Magistrate's
Court-II, Hosdurg. Even if, the case is proceeded with, no
fruitful purpose would be served and therefore, all further
proceedings in C.C.No.924 of 2016 before the Judicial First
Class Magistrate's Court-II, Hosdurg based on Annexure A2
final report in Crime No.404 of 2005 of Bekal Police is liable to
be quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed, and all further
proceedings in C.C.No.924 of 2016 before the Judicial First
Class Magistrate's Court-II, Hosdurg based on Annexure A2
final report in Crime No.404 of 2005 of Bekal Police are

quashed
