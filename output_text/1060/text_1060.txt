JUDGMENT

1. Accused stands trial for offences punishable under sections 323, 427 and 354

of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2. The prosecution case in brief is as follows :- Due to the enmity on
31.12.2017, at 05.00 pm, near the Vilayilkulam — Kazhakkoottam public road,
the accused pushed down CW2, when CWI intervened and tried to lift CW2,
the accused by uttering obscene words hit on the back of CWI, thereby she
dishonoured . It is alleged that the accused beaten all over the body parts of
CWS and caused damage to the gate and thereby CWI sustained loss of

Rs.2000/-. ‘Thus accused committed offences punishable under sections 323,

427 and 354 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. Final report was filed before this court by the Sub Inspector of police Thumba
police station and the case was taken on file as CC.664/2018. Cognizance was
taken for the offences punishable under sections 323, 427 and 354 of Indian

Penal Code, 1860 against the accused.

4. On appearance of the accused, he was enlarged on bail on 04.01.2018. Copies

of all relevant prosecution records were furnished to him under section 207
 306

Code of Criminal Procedure, 1973. After hearing prosecution and counsel for
accused charge framed for offences under sections 323, 427 and 354 of Indian
Penal Code, 1860 read over and explained to the accused to which he pleaded

not guilty and claimed to be tried.

5. On the side of prosecution, PW1 and PW2 were examined and Ext. Pl was
‘marked. As the material witnesses failed to support the prosecution case, the
learned Assistant Public Prosecutor gave up the CW4 to CW10. CW2 reported
