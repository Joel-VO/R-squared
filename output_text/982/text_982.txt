ORDER

2. This MC is filed by the Petitioner herein under Section 12 of the Protection of
Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005 (hereinafter referred to as ‘PWDV Act’)
seeking for various reliefs under the said Act.

3. The averments of the Petitioner in brief is that she has been subjected to acts of
domestic violence in the nature of physical abuse, emotional and verbal abuse and
economic abuse by the Respondent.

4. On issuance of process, the Respondent entered appearance. The parties were
referred for mediation on their request on 21.10.24 and the matter got settled
between the parties on the basis of Mediation Agreement dated 22.11.24, Perused the

terms of Mediation Agreement. Heard both sides on the terms. This Court is satisfied
 that the terms of Mediation Agreement are lawful. and therefore this MC is disposed,
of in terms of the Mediation Agreement. The Mediation Agreement shall form part
of the Order.

5. In the result, this MC is disposed of in terms of the Mediation Agreement. The
‘Mediation Agreement shall form part of the Order,
