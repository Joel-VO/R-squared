ORDER

The petitioner is proceeded against for violation
of the provisions of the Minimum Wages Act, 1948.
According to the petitioner, the private educational
institution is not covered by the provisions of the said Act.

2. The learned Public Prosecutor has invited
the attention of this court to the notification SRO
612/201 dated 12.09.2011 whereby the Government has
notified all the non-teaching staff of private educational
institutions as persons covered by the said Act. Matters
being so, this Crl.M.C is not maintainable and is only to be
dismissed, and | do so.

In the result, this Crl.M.C is dismissed.
Regarding the other contentions taken up by the
petitioner, those contentions can be taken up by the

petitioner before the court below. As far as practicable,
