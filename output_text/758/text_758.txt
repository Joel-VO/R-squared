ORDER

Petitioners are the accused in S.C. No.787/2016
of the Assistant Sessions Court(Additional), Palakkad, which
has arisen from Crime No.619/2016 of Nenmara Police
Station, for the offences punishable under Sections 143,
147, 148, 341, 323, 324 and 308 read with Section 149 IPC.

2. It seems that the matter has been amicably
settled between the parties.

3. On going through the matter, this Court is
satisfied that there are no ingredients to invite an offence
under Section 308 IPC in the matter. The 2” respondent,
who is the de facto complainant, has filed Annexure-A3
affidavit, affirming that the matter has been amicably settled
between him and the petitioners and he has no complaints

against the petitioners.
 2018KERA7I
(CrL.M.C.8827/2017

4. When the matter has been amicably settled
between the parties, no purpose would be served in
proceeding with the matter any further. Matters being so, all
further proceedings against the petitioners in S.C.
No.787/2016 of the Assistant Sessions Court(Additional),
Palakkad, which has arisen from Crime No.619/2016 of
Nenmara Police Station, can be quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings against the petitioners in S.C. No.787/2016 of
the Assistant Sessions Court(Additional), Palakkad, which
has arisen from Crime No.619/2016 of Nenmara Police
Station, are quashed.
