ORDER

1.The petitioner is the 8” accused in S.C No, 370 of 2011
on the file of the Assistant Sessions Court,
Neyyattinkara. He is charged for having committed
offences punishable under Sections 143, 144, 147, 148,
149, 436, 156(A) of the Indian Penal Code. Instant
petition is filed seeking expeditious trial of the
proceedings.

2.When the matter had come up for admission, this
Court had called for a report from the learned Sessions
Judge. The learned Sessions Judge has highlighted the
large pendency of files and has also stated that
directions have been issued by this Court to dispose of
the matters up to the year 2010 by December, 2016. It
is also pointed out that the petitioner was absconding
till 2010 and he has also role to play in protracting the
matter. The learned counsel would submit that the

crime is of the year 2005 and prays that necessary

3.Having regard to the said report, this petition is

a period of 12 months from the date of receipt of a copy

of this order.
