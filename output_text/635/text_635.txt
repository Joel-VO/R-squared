ORDER

The petitioner herein is the accused No.3 in Crime
No.62/1993 of Pazhayangadi Police Station, Kannur for offences
punishable under Sections 143, 147, 457, 380 read with Section
149 of IPC.

2. After investigation, final report was laid and the
remaining accused faced the trial in C.C. No.360/1994. They were
acquitted, evidenced by Annexure Alll judgment. The case
against the petitioner herein was split up and refiled as C.C.
No.76/1998. Since the accused remained absent, matter is now
pending as LP. No.16/2001. The petitioner contends that the

matter has been settled between the parties and in the light of

the settlement as well as Annexure Alll judgment, he is entitled
for a relief and also to get the proceedings quashed.

3. Heard the learned Public Prosecutor who submitted
that the matter has been settled between the parties and
Annexure Alll is not under challenge in any other proceeding.

Learned counsel for the contesting respondents relied on
 2017KERRIS
Cri. M, C. No, 7815 of 2016

Annexures A IV & A V and submitted that the matter has been
settled and they have no grievance against the petitioner herein.
4, In the light of the above, | am satisfied that, no
purpose will be served by prosecuting the petitioner herein.
In the result, Crl.M.C. is allowed. All further proceedings in
Crime No.62/1993 of Pazhayangadi Police Station, Kannur stand

quashed.
