ORDER
Dated this the 06" day of January, 2020

The petitioners are accused in Crime.No.1792/2012 of
Aluva Police Station for having allegedly committed offences
punishable under Section 420 of the IPC and under Section 63 of
the Copy Right Act, 1957. The defacto complainant is a
company impleaded as 2” respondent. The company is
represented by a counsel and has filed affidavit to the effect that
the matter has been amicably settled with the petitioners and
that they do not have any grievance against the petitioners. The
petitioners do not have any criminal antecedents. A report has
been obtained from the Investigating Officer through the learned

Senior Public Prosecutor to the said effect.

In the result, the Crl.MC is allowed and the entire

proceedings as against the petitioners in Crime.No. 1792/2012 of
Aluva Police Station stands quashed under Section 482 of the

Cr-P.C and the accused are discharged.
