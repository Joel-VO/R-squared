ORDER

Alleging offences punishable under Sections 376 and
506(ii) of the Indian Penal Code, a private complaint was
filed before the court below by the 2° respondent. The
matter was forwarded to the police for investigation under
Section 156(3) CrPC. Initially, the police registered the
Crime No.420/2016 through Annexure A-1 FIR. According
to the petitioner and the 2 respondent, the matter stands
settled between them, and the 2% respondent has no
objection in getting the proceedings against the petitioner
quashed.

2. The learned Public Prosecutor submits that the
matter was investigated into and it was found that the
petitioner had not committed the offences punishable under

Sections 376 and 506(ii) IPC. The matter has been referred
 2016KERI665
CRLIM.C.No. 4466 of 2016

by the police. The learned counsel for the petitioner has
produced the attested copy of the report filed by the Circle
Inspector of Police, Town North Circle, Kochi city before the
court below wherein it has been made clear that the
aforesaid offences were not committed in the case and
therefore, the matter has already been referred. When the
matter has been referred by the police and especially when
the parties have come to a settlement, this Court is of the
view that all further proceedings based on Annexure A1 FIR
in Crime No.420/2016 of Emakulam Town North Police
Station, can be quashed.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. is allowed and all further
proceedings based on Annexure A1 FIR in Crime
No.420/2016 of Emakulam Town North Police Station, are
hereby quashed.
