This case having been finally heard today, the court delivered the following:
JUDGMENT

1. Accused stands trial for offences punishable under sections 294(b), 506(i), 323,

and 341 read with 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860.

2. Prosecution case in_brief is as follows:- Due to the previous enmity, on
04.01.2018, at 17.00 hours, Near Thiruvallam Bridge, Puthanpalli Ward,
‘Muttathara Village, accused nos. 1 and 2 wrongfully restrained CW1 and
uttered obscene words and threatened him. It is alleged that accused no.1
slapped on the cheek of CW1. Thus the accused nos. 1 and 2 have committed
offences punishable under sections 294(b), 506(i), 323 and 341 read with 34 of

Indian Penal Code, 1860.

3. Final report was filed before this court by the Sub Inspector of police

Poonthura police station and the case was taken on file as CC. 743/2018.

10, Learned Assistant Public Prosecutor has given up the other witnesses in this
case and she is justified in doing so as the material witness failed to support the
prosecution case. There is nothing on record to disclose that the accused Nos. 1
to 4 have committed the offences as alleged by the prosecution. Having
considered the evidence on record, this court is of the view that the
prosecution has failed to prove the guilt of the accused persons and for the said

reasons, these points are found against the prosecution.

11, Point No, 5:= Upon the findings in Point Nos. 1 to 4, the accused Nos.1 and 2
are found not guilty of offences punishable under sections 294(b), 506(i), 323
and 341 read with 34 of Indian Penal Code, 1860 and they are acquitted of the
said offences under section 255(1) Code of Criminal Procedure.1973. Their
bail bonds stands cancelled and they are set at liberty.
