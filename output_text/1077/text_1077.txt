ORDER

1. Accused stands charged with the offences punishable w/s. 55 (i) of Kerala
Abkari Act.

2. Prosecution case in brief is as follows:- On 04.11.2024, at 18.00 hours, at
Naduthara Beach, Poonthura Ward, Muttathara Village, accused was found as
selling 5.75 litres of Indian Made Foreign Liquor and sale proceeds of
%.4,370/-, in contravention of the existing law. Thus the accused has
committed offences punishable w/s. 55 (i) of Kerala Abkari Act.

3. On filing final report, the case was taken on file as CP No. 67/2024. When
process served, the accused person entered appearance. He was enlarged on
bail during crime stage and allowed to continue in the same. Copies of all
prosecution records were furnished him as provided under section 230 of
Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha Sanhita, 2023.

4. On perusal of the case records, it appears to me that the offences alleged

against the accused is exclusively triable by the Hon'ble Sessions Court.
 2082

Hence, the case is to be committed to the Hon'ble Sessions Court,
Thiruvananthapuram. Therefore, the case is hereby committed to the Hon’ble
Sessions Court, Thiruvananthapuram w/s.232 of Bharatiya Nagarik Suraksha
Sanhita, 2023.

5. Issue notice to the Public Prosecutor. Notify him of the committal of the case
and transmit records and material objects to the Hon’ble Sessions Court as per
Rules.

6. The accused shall appear before the Hon'ble Court of Sessions,
‘Thiruvananthapuram on issuance of summons.
