Heard, perused the records and passed the following:

JUDGMENT

1. The accused stands charge sheeted by the Sub Inspector of Police, Museum Police
Station alleging offence U/Ss. 279,337 and 338 of Indian Penal Code.

2. Accused pleaded guilty through counsel. Particulars of offence U/Ss. 279,337 and
338 of Indian Penal Code was read over and explained to the accused and he
pleaded guilty through counsel. This court is convinced that the plea of the
accused is voluntary. Hence his plea is accepted. The accused is found guilty for
 2

the offence U/S. 279,337 and 338 of Indian Penal Code and he is
convicted thereunder.

Accused is convicted and sentenced to pay a fine of Rs. 1,500/- for the
offence U/S.279,337 and 338 of Indian Penal Code. In default of payment
of fine the accused shall undergo simple imprisonment for 10 days.
