ORDER
The petitioners are accused in Crime No. 401 of 2018
of Adhur Police Station registered for the offences
punishable under Sections 143, 147, 148, 323, 324 and 506
read with Section 149 IPC and Section 3 (1)(s) of
Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes (Prevention of
Atrocities) Amendment Act, 2015.

2. The first respondent is the de facto complainant,
who filed affidavit stating that the matter has been settled
and hence he has no further grievance against the
petitioners. The learned Public Prosecutor, on instructions,
has also submitted that the matter has been settled by the
parties. The offences alleged are personal in nature.
Therefore, settlement of the above dispute will lead to
peace and harmony in the locality. In the said
circumstances, I am inclined to quash Annexure-Al FIR

and further proceedings against the petitioners in Crime
 crl.MC.No. 8717 of 2018

*
No. 401 of 2018 of Adhur Police Station in exercise of the
inherent power under Section 482 Cr.P.C., to meet the ends

offustice, It is ordered accordingly.

In the result, this Crl.M.C. stands allowed.
