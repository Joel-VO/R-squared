ORDER

Petitioner is the third accused in C.C No. 595/2009 on the
file of the Additional Chief Judicial Magistrate Court,

‘Thiruvananthapuram. The offence alleged is punishable under Sect

mn
16 of the Kerala Anti-Social Activities Prevention Act, 2007 and Section
34 of IPC. The cognizance of the case was taken on 21.12.2009. All the
accused appeared and took bail on 15,092011, The Cri.M.C is filed
seeking a direction to the Addl.Chief Judicial Magistrate,
‘Thiruvananthapuram to expedite the trial in C. C No. 595/209 and to
dispose of the same at an earlier date.

2. The Addl. Chief Judicial Magistrate, Thiruvananthapuram in
the report dated 18.11.2013 reported that the case is posted to
18.02.2014 for framing charge that, prosecution has cited 27 witnesses
in the said case and that four months time is necessary for disposal of
case after framing charge.

In the light of the report, the Crl.M.C is disposed of
directing the Addl.Chief Judicial Magistrate, Thiruvananthapuram to
expedite the trial in C.C No. 595/09 and to dispose of the case within a

period of four months from the date of framing the charge.
