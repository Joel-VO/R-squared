ORDER

1.This petition is filed under section 482 of the Code of
Criminal Procedure (hereinafter referred to as 'the Code’
for brevity)

2.The petitioner is the registered owner of a mini lorry
bearing registration No.KL-23L/6815. The said vehicle
was seized on 23.8.2016 by the 2” respondent on the
allegation that the same was used for the transportation
of red earth, The grievance of the petitioner is that the
seizure of the vehicle has not been reported before the
learned Magistrate.

3.1 have heard the learned counsel for the petitioner as well
as the learned Public Prosecutor.

4.It is submitted by the learned counsel that though the

vehicle was seized under the provisions of the Kerala

 2016KERIA2232

CAM.C.6532/2016 2
Minor Mineral Concession Rules, 2015 read with the Mines
and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957,
the seizure of the vehicle has not been reported till date
before the jurisdictional Magistrate.

5.The petitioner seeks for a direction to the 2" respondent
to report the seizure of the aforesaid vehicle and to issue
appropriate directions to the learned Magistrate to release
the vehicle. The learned counsel refers to orders dated
1.9.2015 in W.A.1889/2015 and 1890/2015 and order
dated 14.6.2016 in W.A.1126 of 2016 and 1129 of 2016 to
contend that a Division Bench of this Court, in an appeal
challenging the provisions, has passed interim orders
directing release of the vehicle subject to conditions.
Prayer is to direct release of the vehicle on the same
conditions.

6.Having regard to the facts and circumstances, the 2”
respondent is directed to report the seizure of the vehicle
bearing registration _No.KL-23L/6815 before —_the

jurisdictional Magistrate and the learned Magistrate is
